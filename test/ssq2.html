SSQ1 Conceptual model

Specification Model

 The arrival time of job i is ai.
 The delay of job i in the queue is di  0.
 The time that job i begins service is bi = ai +di.
 The service time of job i is si > 0.
 The wait of job i in the service node (queue and service) is wi = di +si.
 The time that job i completes service (the departure time) is ci = ai + wi.
